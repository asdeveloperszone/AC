Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I get a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours. During peak times, it may take slightly longer, but we'll do our best to get back to you as soon as possible.

Can I suggest new tutorial topics?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our community. If you have suggestions for new tutorials or topics you'd like to see covered, please use the "Tutorial Request" subject in the contact form.

How do I report a bug or error?

If you find a bug or error in our code examples or website, please email us at bugs@ccode.com or use the "Bug Report" option in the contact form. Include as much detail as possible to help us fix it quickly.

Are you available for collaborations?

Yes! We're open to partnerships and collaborations that align with our mission of providing quality programming education. Please select "Partnership/Collaboration" in the contact form to discuss opportunities.

Can I contribute content to CCODE?

We appreciate community contributions! If you're interested in contributing tutorials, exercises, or other content, please reach out to us with details about what you'd like to contribute.
